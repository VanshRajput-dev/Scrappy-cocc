    • Kawasaki KLX230
      Kawasaki KLX 230 Review: Perfect Trail Bike, Decent for Daily Use
      5 hrs ago
      Sanjay B S
      Ive ridden the Kawasaki KLX 230 for around 4000 km, mainly on off-road trails, bad roads, city rides, and a few long rides. The bike really excels off-road. Lightweight, great suspension, and smooth power delivery make trail riding easy and confidence-inspiring. It handles mud, gravel, and rough terrain very well. On long rides and highways, the experience is average. Comfortable till around 8090 km/h, but the seat and vibrations make longer highway rides tiring. City riding is easy due to its nimble handling, though the tall seat height may be an issue for some. Overall, its a great dual-sport bike for off-road and trail lovers, reliable and fun, but not ideal if highway touring is your main priority.
